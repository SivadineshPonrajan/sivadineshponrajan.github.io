Gesture based drone control

Used MPU6050 (MEMS Module) to read the IMU data and sent them serially to the pc. Whenever I move or turn it, the axis was calculated and the Tello drone is instructed via the socket connection from the PC to move using its SDK.

Components used: Tello Drone | MPU6050

Bi-Copter

Designed and implemented the closed-loop control feedback mechanism (PID) to control the BLDC motors in order to maintain the balance by calibrating the motor speed and by calculating the angle using the MPU6050 - MEMS module.

Components used: Arduino Uno R3 | MPU6050

InMoov robotic arm with leap motion

3D printed and assembled the open source prosthetic arm called InMoov with some design changes. Controlled the arm based on the inputs from leap motion sensor. Also tried with computer vision to detect the hand posture from live webcam feed.

Sensor used: Leap Motion | Computer vision library: OpenCV

Depth Estimation with Transfer Learning

Implemented the monocular depth estimation via transfer learning with Tensorflow and Keras. The data was collected from the lidar and the features were extracted using high performing pre-trained networks on initializing the encoder with augmentation. Thus accurate results with higher resolution was obtained.
